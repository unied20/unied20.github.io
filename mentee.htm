MENTEES.



The college application process is cumbersome. You have to fill out your personal information in repetitive forms, shortlist universities, prepare & give standardised tests, write essays, prepare for interviews and simultaneously complete your high school coursework.

At such a time, an experienced mentor is what you need. Having gone through this process just last year, UNi-ED is something we wish we had.

UNi-ED also assigns you a mentor who we know can help you; we use an algorithm to scour our database for a mentor that shares your interests and ambitions.

We believe UNi-ED can genuinely make a difference.
