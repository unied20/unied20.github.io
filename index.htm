THE UNi-ED MISSION

UNi-ED is a free service that fosters mentorship between college and high school seniors to make the process of applying to college clear by making necessary knowledge equitable.

EQUITABLE.

By working towards undoing the information asymmetry the 'college application industry' has generated by monetizing information, endowing wealthier students with more knowledge and a meritless advantage.

CLEAR.

By nurturing a student-to-student relationship to enable the flow of unadulterated, accurate information.




WHAT'S UNIQUE

Our commitment to the idea that knowledge should be equitable makes us unique. To meet this ideal, we promise UNi-ED is free-to-use and includes no hidden costs.

We want our mentees to make more informed decisions on their own. To do this, we operate on a student-to-student model of communication with UNi-ED as an intermediary. A mentee’s agency is of paramount importance to our mission, equity through clarity.

HOW IT WORKS

UNi-ED relies on a crowdsourced database of verified college students to make the mission work. When we receive your application to be a mentee, we introduce your information to this database. Our algorithms find your ideal match and we connect you with your mentor.

After this, our mentors take over. They use our mentor tools to provide impactful and ethical advice.
