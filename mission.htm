WHAT'S

   

UNIQUE.


UNi-ED is a non-profit that allows high school student to leverage the experience of current college students towards a greater understanding of the various university application processes.

UNi-ED is completely free-to-use and includes no hidden costs! We know it sounds too good to be true, but we believe the lack of a monetary incentive to our mentors means we attract only those who believe in our ethos.

We want our mentees to make more informed decisions on their own. To do this, we operate on a model where students interact with verified mentors who help them understand and evaluate decisions, not make them.
