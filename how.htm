HOW IT

   

WORKS.

Once you sign-up to receive mentorship, we introduce your information to our database of verified college students. Then, we use our algorithms to find your ideal mentor. Our algorithm uses several variables to ensure we find a mentor who is most suited to your needs.

Before we connect the mentor and mentee, we review the match to ensure it truly is the best.

To ensure our mentorship process is ethical and structured, we introduced ‘mentor tools’. These tools help ensure all the help they provide the mentee is impactful and ethical. This reaffirms the idea that a UNi-ED mentee must make their own decisions, albeit better informed.

UNi-ED is a rapidly growing organisation. During this time, our systems and technology are continuously evolving as we serve more students, but our focus is resolute; Equity through Clarity.
